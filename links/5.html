p2: This group differs only from p1 in that is contains 180° rotations, that is, rotations of order 2. As in all symmetry groups there are translations, but there are neither reflections nor glide reflections. The two translations axes may be inclined at any angle to each other.




The Alhambra Project is grounded in stories and histories of residents and merchants in the neighborhood surrounding the Alhambra Theater. Neighborhood pets, artists, writers and merchants, make their appearance both inside the theater and in the walking tour around the neighborhood.

Lynn Marie Kirby and Christoph Steger: Neighborhood Poster, 2016 One of four posters displayed on the exterior of the Alhambra Theater. One in a series of early project sketches. It features the voices of geologist Doris Sloan and the artist/designer Mark Ong, and a walk up Green Street to the site of photographer Imogen Cunningham’s house.
