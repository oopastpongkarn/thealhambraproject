Many types of music form the mosaic of sound in the Alhambra Project: Debussy’s The Wine Gate, played by Carl Blake, Mamoru Fujieda’s Patterns of Plants, and XX’s flamenco guitar, and all the snippets of film scores that once played in the theater. Site recordings from both Alhambras, water recording from the Bay and the Alhambra Palace fountains, singing, and calls to pray add texture to the arrangement.

These animations are based on tessellation designs in the Alhambra Palace and are set to Mamoru Fujieda’s Patterns of Plants.
