On September 30, 2016, from 4PM - 10PM The Alhambra Project was launched as a series of choreographed activities in the neighborhood surrounding the Alhambra Theater. Photographer Minoosh Zomorodinia documented the event, please view a selection of images, and reviews for a sense of the evening.
