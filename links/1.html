(two) Alhambras

p1: This is the simplest symmetry group. It consists only of translations. There are neither reflections, glide-reflections, nor rotations. The two translations axes may be inclined at any angle to each other.
The Alhambra Theater in San Francisco was modeled after the Alhambra Palace in Granada, Spain — an architectural wonder of the Moorish empire and a testament to the coexistence of Muslim, Jewish, and Christian traditions in Europe. The Alhambra Project always looks at both Alhambras.
