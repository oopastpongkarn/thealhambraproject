Both Alhambras and the Russian Hill neighborhood are associated with many writers. Federico Garcia Lorca, and Washington Irving wrote while in residence at the Alhambra palace. Frank Norris, ( see symbol 5) Jade Snow Wong, ( see symbol 5) and Ina Coolbrith ( see symbol 5) lived in Russian Hill/ Polk Gulch and wrote about the neighborhood. Their presence and influence can be felt throughout the project.
