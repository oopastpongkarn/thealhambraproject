Inspired by the Alhambra Palace with its splendid water fountains and underground water systems, water is a large part of the Alhambra Project. The Alhambra fountains are featured on gym monitors, with water sounds playing over speakers near the gym’s own water fountains. Cecile Marie swims daily in the Bay near the theater and made a film about it, Donna Schumacher recorded herself swimming in the Bay. Binta Ayofemi presents a found waterfall video loop that was installed near stationary bikes.

One in a series of early project sketches. It features a clip from Cecile Marie’s swimming film and early animation experiments with Alhambra patterns. Film scholar and art historian Laura Marks talks about the Andalusian influence in the Alhambra. The music Patterns of Plants is by composer Mamoru Fujieda.
